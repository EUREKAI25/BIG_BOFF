
I want to see myself in a bright, inspiring place, abroad or somewhere different from here.
A large terrace or balcony with an open view, overlooking the sea or the city.
I am seated at a table, an open notebook in front of me, a coffee beside it.

I am wearing simple, elegant clothing.
I feel calm, relaxed, fully present.

I want it to be clearly visible that I am no longer in my usual daily life,
that I have changed environments,
and that I feel exactly where I belong in this new setting.
