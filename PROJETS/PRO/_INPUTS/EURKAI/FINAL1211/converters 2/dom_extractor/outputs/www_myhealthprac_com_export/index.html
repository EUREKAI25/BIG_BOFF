Quick Answers

How do I join MyHealthPrac?

You can join our priority waitlist in under 60 seconds.

As we prepare for launch, early access members will be the first to experience a clinical-grade platform that combines diagnostic intelligence with practitioner-level precision at exclusive prices … before the rest of the industry catches up

Who is MyHealthPrac for?

MyHealthPrac is built for health professionals who want to truly deliver personalised results from the latest research. Whether you’re a coach, clinician, or multidisciplinary team, the platform gives you access to over 290 biomarkers, nearly triple what most platforms offer, and maps them against nearly 200 predictive patterns, curated from functional ranges developed from 16 years experience and insights from over 3,000 peer-reviewed medical journals.

While others stop at tracking or templated advice, MyHealthPrac empowers you to interpret data like a clinical specialist, personalise protocols in real time, and scale outcomes across teams – without relying on outdated data, inaccurate claims or static dashboards.

What is Functional Medicine?

Functional medicine considers everything that affects your health — both inside and outside the body. It’s the integration of cutting-edge medical science with traditional philosophies, applied through the lens of systems biology. 

Rather than just managing symptoms, it aims to solve the root cause by looking at how your biology, nutrition, environment, lifestyle, and history all interact. 

It’s where science meets strategy, and where healing becomes personal.

Where is MyHealthPrac available?

MyHealthPrac operates globally, with full access to our exclusive practitioner-led community, providing 24/7 remote support. We’ve integrated every clinical unit of measure into functional ranges, across countries and regions, making health truly borderless.

MyHealthPrac enables you to generate a comprehensive, clinically intelligent report in minutes, no matter where in the world the panel comes from. Unlike platforms tied to one country or lab provider, we’re built for cross-border care, multilingual teams, and a future where location should never limit health outcomes.

When’s the best time to start using MyHealthPrac?

The best time is before your clients show symptoms. MyHealthPrac was built for practitioners who believe prevention is smarter than reaction.

Our platform doesn’t just track health, it anticipates dysfunction through predictive pattern recognition, giving you the tools to act earlier, intervene smarter, and deliver better outcomes. Most platforms wait for problems. We help you prevent them.

Do I need to be a doctor to use MyHealthPrac?

Our first release is exclusively for registered health professionals, including functional medicine practitioners, clinicians, health coaches, and allied health providers. You don’t need to be a doctor, but you do need to be qualified.

MyHealthPrac was built to elevate practitioner care first, equipping professionals with clinical-grade tools, predictive intelligence, and time-saving automation. Public access will come in future phases, once we’ve ensured the infrastructure supports the same level of precision and impact for individuals worldwide.

What makes MyHealthPrac different from other health platforms?

Most platforms focus on tracking markers, MyHealthPrac goes several steps further, delivering clinical-grade interpretation, practitioner workflows, and over 290 markers, verified across the bioindiviudal ranges representing the best health outcomes, which is then mapped against nearly 200 predictive patterns, all drawn from 3,000+ medical journals. It’s built for outcomes, not just overviews.

What features are included in MyHealthPrac?

MyHealthPrac includes intelligent lab analysis, functional pattern recognition, client management dashboards, practitioner note systems, report automation, and data visualisation — all in one system.

Think of it as your clinical brain and admin assistant combined into one.

How does MyHealthPrac interpret test results and data?

We don’t just display data, we decode it with precision. MyHealthPrac’s interpretation engine is built from over 16 years of clinical expertise and carefully constructed over three years of rigorous development to ensure care, validity and accuracy. We’ve integrated insights from thousands of peer-reviewed medical journals, each selected through stringent inclusion criteria.

While many platforms outsource interpretation to generic AI models or external labs, risking inconsistency, vulnerabilities and clinical inaccuracy, our algorithm is practitioner-built and evidence-backed, mapping over 290 biomarkers into functional patterns, red flags, and system-level dysfunctions. It’s not just tech, it’s decades of clinical intelligence, engineered into minutes.

Can I use MyHealthPrac to deliver remote care at scale, not just monitor my own health?

Absolutely. MyHealthPrac was built for professionals managing dozens to thousands of clients, with tools for team-based care, time-saving workflows, and automated reporting. It’s not just scalable, it’s structured for sustainable clinical growth without sacrificing intimacy or impact.

Is MyHealthPrac built by actual practitioners, not just software engineers or influencers?

Yes. MyHealthPrac is led by practitioners who serve over 20,000 clients annually, not by content creators or tech founders with no clinical background.

Every feature is built from lived experience, with precision, safety, and outcomes at the core.

Does MyHealthPrac replace my doctor or practitioner?

No. MyHealthPrac enhances your relationship with qualified health professionals.

It doesn’t diagnose or replace clinical oversight, it empowers practitioners to detect patterns earlier, personalise care faster, and explain results with confidence.

Can MyHealthPrac explain why I feel the way I do?

Yes, and with more precision than generic lab comments or symptom checkers.

By analysing biomarkers, symptom clusters, and timeline data together, MyHealthPrac reveals why you’re experiencing dysfunction, and what to focus on next.

Are test results explained in detail?

Every result comes with clear insights, contextual explanations, and functional patterns. No more cryptic numbers or Google rabbit holes, just meaningful, clinically useful information you can act on.

What if I’ve already completed labs elsewhere?

No problem. You can upload past results, and MyHealthPrac will automatically convert them, regardless of country, lab provider, or units, into an interpretable, structured report in minutes.

How does MyHealthPrac protect my information?

We adhere to industry-leading privacy standards, including HL7 compatibility and ISO27001-level encryption. Your data is stored securely, and always controlled by you and your practitioner.

Can MyHealthPrac diagnose conditions?

No. While MyHealthPrac identifies patterns, flags risk, and explains dysfunction, it does not diagnose diseases.

We support clinical decision-making, but the final diagnosis and treatment always rest with a qualified healthcare provider.

What happens if there’s something urgent in the results?

If a result exceeds safe thresholds or suggests acute risk, the system will automatically flag it for immediate practitioner review. You won’t be left guessing, our system is designed with early intervention in mind.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. We offer flexible subscriptions so you can pause, cancel, or restart based on your clinic’s needs, no lock-ins, no hidden fees. We’re here to support your growth, not restrict it.
